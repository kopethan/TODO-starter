# Product Overview

## What TODO is

TODO is a trust-aware decision platform where users can:
- understand an object, service, seller, app, organization, or situation
- learn how it normally works
- recognize dangers and red flags
- read structured experience reports
- find signals that suggest risk, anomalies, or recurring patterns
- review supporting sources tied to the right entity

TODO is **not** primarily a social feed and should not feel like a noisy dashboard, entertainment app, or casual posting platform.

## Product character

The product should feel:
- serious
- structured
- minimal
- editorial
- trustworthy
- productivity-oriented, but not sterile

## Recommended content model

The product should be **entity-first**.

That means:
- the main feed defaults to entities
- entity cards can show summary counts and small previews
- reports and signals are primarily explored within the entity detail page
- sources support the entity detail page rather than competing with it
- optional alternate routes for reports/signals can exist later, but should not dominate the primary public flow

## Recommended V1 navigation concept

Public-facing experience:
- Feed
- Entity detail with tabs
- Search / Filter / Ask AI
- Share information

Notes:
- `/reports` and `/signals` may still exist as secondary routes
- they should not dominate primary public navigation
- the entity page is the main depth surface for understanding reports, signals, and sources

Admin-facing experience:
- Entities
- Reports
- Contributions intake
- Moderation queue
- AI-suggested updates for validation

## Product goals

1. Help users decide whether something is safe, trustworthy, or risky.
2. Help users understand context, not just consume isolated complaints.
3. Keep evidence, patterns, and experiences tied to the right subject.
4. Use AI as a guide and summarizer, not as an ungrounded content generator.
5. Make public information sharing structured enough to stay reviewable and trustworthy.
