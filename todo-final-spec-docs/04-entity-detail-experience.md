# Entity Detail Experience

## Purpose

The entity detail page is where users go from discovery to understanding.

It should answer:
- what this entity is
- how it normally works
- what the main red flags are
- what people have experienced
- what patterns or signals matter
- what sources support the understanding
- what matters for the user's specific situation

## Recommended page structure

The entity detail page should be the main public depth surface.

Recommended top-level tabs:
1. Overview
2. Reports
3. Signals
4. Sources

Recommended behavior:
- default to **Overview**
- keep the reading flow centered and moderately wide
- remove unnecessary side navigation once tabs are present
- keep reports/signals/sources subordinate to the entity rather than feeling like separate products

## Recommended content inside Overview

Overview can still contain multiple grounded subsections, such as:
- what this entity is
- how it works
- risks and red flags
- important notes
- Ask AI entry point

## Why reports and signals belong here

Reports and signals are most useful when users already know:
- what entity they are looking at
- what the normal context is
- how the reported experiences fit the broader picture

Therefore:
- feed = discovery
- entity detail = interpretation and depth

## Reports tab

Recommended features:
- count
- filter by topic/severity/time
- structured cards
- evidence indicators
- verification/moderation indicators where appropriate
- AI summary of selected or all reports

## Signals tab

Recommended features:
- active signals
- recent signals
- grouped signals by theme
- AI explanation of what signals mean
- confidence / strength hints where appropriate

## Sources tab

Recommended features:
- list of supporting sources
- timestamps
- type/source origin
- optional source summaries
- section links showing what each source supports

## Images

Images are useful when the entity is:
- a product
- an app
- a website
- a place
- a visually identifiable object
- a storefront or listing
- evidence-driven subject

Images should be contextual, not decorative.

## AI on entity detail

This is the strongest place for AI.

Example prompts:
- summarize this entity for me
- what are the biggest red flags
- summarize refund-related complaints
- compare positive and negative patterns
- what should I check before using this
- what matters for my situation
